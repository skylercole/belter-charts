# Credits & data sources

This is unofficial, non-commercial fan work. The Expanse was created by
James S. A. Corey; the television series is produced by Alcon Entertainment.
No show assets are used.

## Data
- **JPL Horizons** (NASA/JPL Solar System Dynamics) — small-body state
  vectors for Ceres, Pallas, Juno, Vesta, Hygiea and Eros, 2340-2365.
  https://ssd.jpl.nasa.gov/horizons/
- **astronomy-engine** by Don Cross (MIT license) — planetary positions.
  https://github.com/cosinekitty/astronomy

## Conventions
- Tycho / Anderson station placement follows IanH's Expanse wargame
  calculator conventions until canon research supersedes them.
